Podcast

In our podcast, our group explored the role of women in Quentin Tarantino's latest film, Once Upon a Time in Hollywood. We delve into the different women depicted in the movie and what their portryal can tell us about how Tarantino, and Hollywood, feel about women.

Video

Our video focuses on the idea of an actor's, or any performer's, "peak" of his or her career. This is an idea that we were very interested in, and upon researching it, discovered there is no one definable peak for any performer. We delve into our interpretations of what it means to "peak".
